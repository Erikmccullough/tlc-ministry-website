Feed bellies. Feed souls. Build futures.

We gather around tacos, art and compassion. Our mission is to nurture those in need—especially at‑risk youth—as they navigate a world transformed by artificial intelligence.

TLC Ministry is committed to fostering creative, compassionate solutions that nourish and uplift our community. We recognize that automation is reshaping work; we help those at risk of displacement adapt and thrive. Through meals, art, mentoring and technology, we create spaces where everyone can develop skills, discover purpose and connect. Our goal is simple: no one left behind.

About Us

TLC Ministry uses tacos, love and creativity to uplift our community. We host monthly Taco Night dinners, art workshops and technology labs that encourage connection, compassion and resilience. Some of our programs are up and running; others are ambitious dreams. We are transparent about what exists today and what is coming tomorrow.

We emphasize transparency: our organization is a 501(c)(3) in formation and we share our progress openly. Your support helps us turn dreams into reality.

Donate

Your generosity nourishes our community and empowers future creators.

All online donations are processed securely through our hosted PayPal giving button and can be configured to auto-transfer into TLC Ministry's linked bank account.

For direct ACH/Wire gifts to the TLC bank account, email erik@tacosloveandcreation.org for current routing instructions.

Why Give?

Your support funds meals, supplies and workshops. As AI changes the job landscape, donations help us train youth with creative and technical skills so they can thrive.

GMC TV Funding Campaign

TLC Ministry is seeking designated donations for a GMC TV production and distribution initiative that will amplify testimonies, youth stories, and community transformation.

Campaign gifts will support filming, editing, broadcast readiness, captioning, and local outreach events connected to each release.
