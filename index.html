Our mission

We believe creativity is a survival skill. Through tacos (yes, tacos), mutual aid, and hands-on art, TLC builds spaces where people feel seen, supported, and inspired.

  • Weekly community meals
  • Open studio nights and youth art labs
  • Public murals and creative placemaking
  • Artist microgrants & materials bank

What makes us different

We combine direct service (food, supplies, care) with creative practice (art builds, workshops, apprenticeships). People don’t just receive help—they co-create the beautiful world they want to live in.
