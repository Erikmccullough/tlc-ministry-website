Our Story

Love in Action. Art in Service. Tacos for All.

TLC Ministry started with a folding table, a griddle, and a simple belief: everyone deserves a full plate and a place to belong. Today we're a growing community of neighbors, artists, mentors, and taco-lovers building something real together in Norman and Oklahoma City.

How We Show Up for Our Community

A People-First Approach

Technology Should Serve People. So Should We.

We're guided by the same principle driving efforts like OpenAI's People-First AI Fund: AI should help solve humanity's hardest problems, and it works best when built with communities, not for them.

01

Community-Led

Neighbors help design and lead every program — decisions are made with the people they serve, not for them.

02

AI Literacy for All

Our AI Pathways mentors help youth and families build the knowledge and confidence to navigate a fast-changing economy.

03

Enhance, Not Replace

We believe technology should strengthen human connection and creative work — never replace the people doing it.

04

Broadly Shared Benefit

We focus on communities too often left out of digital opportunity, so progress reaches everyone — not just the few.
