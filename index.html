Who We Are

TLC Ministry – Tacos, Love & Creation Foundation is a not-for-profit corporation registered in the State of Oklahoma on December 18, 2024. We exist to feed people, build community, and create art that heals. We focus on people experiencing homelessness, economic instability, or AI-driven job loss — especially youth.

What Drives Us

Confidence is a tool. When someone feels seen, fed, warm, and not ashamed, they can show up to build something better with us. We use meals, creative work, and storytelling to open that door.

How We Tell the Story

We produce docu-style video, reels, photos, and on-the-ground reporting from each community we visit. Chapter one is filmed in DFW.
