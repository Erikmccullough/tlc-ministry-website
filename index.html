Feed bellies. Feed souls. Make art together.

TLC Ministry – Tacos, Love & Creation Foundation serves our community with warm meals, creative workshops, and collaborative projects that reconnect people to purpose.

About Us

We are a grassroots organization based in Norman, Oklahoma, dedicated to nurturing community through food and art. We share weekly meals, run creative workshops, and build spaces where people feel seen, supported, and inspired.

Programs

Taco Tuesdays

Every Tuesday 6–8pm: share a hot meal and good vibes. All are welcome.

Creation Lab

Open studio for all ages. Materials provided; curiosity required.

  • Printmaking, denim upcycling, and LED art workshops

Walls of Love

We collaborate with local artists to paint murals and share community stories. Apply to host a mural or sponsor a wall.
