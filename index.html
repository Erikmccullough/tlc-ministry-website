Our Mission

TLC Ministry - Tacos, Love & Creation Foundation operates as early responders to the AI displacement crisis, providing immediate community-based intervention for the unprecedented economic emergency facing working families.

As one of the first organizations specifically addressing AI workforce displacement, we demonstrate that affected communities can lead rapid response efforts that combine immediate mutual aid with long-term organizing for economic justice.
