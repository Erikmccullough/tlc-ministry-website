Feed bellies. Feed souls. Make art together.

We’re a grassroots art + compassion ministry based in Oklahoma. We serve hot food, create safe creative spaces, and help people – especially youth and folks in crisis – build dignity, skill, and community in a world getting rearranged by AI and automation.

Support the work See programs

What we actually do in real life

Food & Presence

Street-level meals. Tacos, water, conversation, eye contact. People are not “projects.” People are people.

Art & Creation Labs

Pop-up art tables, denim upcycling, spray art, drawing, sculpture. Creation is medicine. We hand it out.

Next-Gen Skills

Helping youth prepare for an AI-heavy future: creative problem solving, self-led income streams, and “your creativity is not optional, it’s survival.”

Fuel the mission
