Three Pillars, One Mission

Our Programs

Every program is designed with our community, not just for it — feeding bellies, feeding souls, and building futures side by side.

Pillar One

Tacos: Community Meal Nights

Every week, we set up tables and griddles in Norman and Oklahoma City and cook for anyone who's hungry — no questions, no forms, no judgment. It's a simple act with a big effect: strangers become neighbors over a shared plate.

  • 🌮 Free weekly community meals
  • 🌮 Open to everyone, no eligibility screening
  • 🌮 Powered by volunteer cooks and donated ingredients
Pillar Two

Creation: Art & Mural Workshops

Art is healing, and creating something beautiful together builds pride in a place. Our workshops turn blank walls into murals, scraps of denim into new creations, and idle afternoons into moments of shared joy.

  • 🎨 Community mural painting days
  • 🎨 Upcycled denim & textile art workshops
  • 🎨 Open studio time for all ages
Pillar Three

AI Pathways: Future-Ready Mentorship

As automation and AI reshape the job market, we've seen too many neighbors — especially at-risk youth — get left behind. AI Pathways pairs young people with trusted local mentors who teach digital literacy, practical AI skills, and the confidence to navigate whatever comes next.

  • 💡 One-on-one mentorship with local leaders
  • 💡 Hands-on AI & digital literacy training
  • 💡 Built with — not for — the youth we serve

This program is directly inspired by the people-first principles behind initiatives like OpenAI's People-First AI Fund: technology should enhance human work, and its benefits should reach everyone — not just the few.

Want to Get Involved?

Volunteer at a taco night, join a mural day, or mentor a young person through AI Pathways — there's a role for everyone.
